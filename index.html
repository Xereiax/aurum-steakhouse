The House of AURUM

A temple to fire,
craft & patience.

For a decade, AURUM has stood as Dubai's altar to the perfect cut. We source grass-fed and grain-finished beef from the world's most revered ranches — Hokkaido, the Australian highlands, the prairies of Nebraska — and age each loin in our salt-walled chambers until it reaches singular depth.

Our open-flame Josper grill burns at 350°C, charring the exterior to lacquered gold while the centre stays impossibly tender. Nothing here is rushed. Everything here is earned.
